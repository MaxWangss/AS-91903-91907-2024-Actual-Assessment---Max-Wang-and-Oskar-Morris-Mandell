Who are we?

Red Beach Outrigger Canoe Club is a young up and coming Waka Ama club that was established in 2018 to lead the development and practice of Waka Ama, With a key focus to promote kaupapa waka as a positive activity to support cultural and physical wellbeing within the community.

Waka Ama has proven to be a growing team sport across New Zealand, The sport presents a rich history where identity, tradition and community is at its core and as a result, for many, Waka Ama is not just a sport but also a lifestyle.Â 

Red Beach Outrigger Canoe Club was established in 2018 and is situated in Red Beach Whangaparoa, Auckland New Zealand.

- Our Vision

Develop, educate, practice, and promote all aspects of Waka Ama so that it becomes the leading choice of water sport.

- Our Mission

To grow the Red Beach Outrigger Canoe Club through inclusive on water activities for our community whilst promoting safety, education, health and fitness.

Our Values

Manaakitanga

We encourage hospitality, kindness and generosity through sharing of ourselves and our resources nurturing all people and accepting our differences.

Hauora

We believe in the four dimensions of Hauora which promote the physical, mental, emotional and spiritual well being of all our participants.

Whanaungatanga

Is our sense of belonging, identification and collective strength.

Wairuatanga

Wairuatanga meaning being in touch with the spiritual element, including respect and love for the ocean. To look after each other on and off the water

Tu Tangata

We are accountable for our actions, we have respect for each other, our resources and our environment.
