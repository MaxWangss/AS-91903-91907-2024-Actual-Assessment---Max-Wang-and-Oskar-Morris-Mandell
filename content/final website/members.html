Club Portal

Red Beach Waka Ama Club expects all registered members to commit a certain amount of time and dedication towards activities that the club organizes and involves itself in.

As a member of the club your responsibilities are to:

  • Foster the visions, values and objectives of the club.
  • Maintain the integrity of the Club at all times.
  • Commit to your team and be a team player.
  • Treat all of the clubs equipment with care and report any damages as per the appropriate guidelines.
  • Abide by the safety rules and regulations.
  • Treat all members of the club with courtesy and respect.
  • Participate in social hui events.
  • Assist in fundraising activities.
  • Pay membership fees on time.

    Fees

    Red Beach Outrigger Canoe Club Yearly Fees: $100

    As a paid member of the Red Beach Outrigger Canoe Club, your fees go towards:

  • Insurance of all club assets
  • Maintenance on all club owned canoes
  • Registration fees and maintenance fees of all club trailers
  • Waka Trolley maintenance
  • Club Running Expenses
  • Affiliation fee for WANZ and AROCA

    • Nga Kaihoe O Aotearoa (WANZ or Waka Ama NZ)

    WANZ is our governing National Body for Waka Ama, and the WANZ fee is an annual fee payable per paddler per year to this Organisation.  As an affiliated club, it is our responsibility to collect the WANZ fee off each club member and pass it on to WANZ. 

    WANZ fees below:

    WANZ Fee Structure:

    $15 per Open/Senior (20 years and over)

    $12 per Junior (14 to 19 years)

    $5 per Midget/Intermediate (13 years and under)

    Auckland Region Outrigger Canoe Association (AROCA)

    AROCA is the regional body for Waka Ama in Auckland and the AROCA fee is an annual fee payable per paddler per year to this organisation as of October 2016.  As an affiliated club, it is our responsibility to collect the AROCA fee off each club member and pass this onto AROCA.

    AROCA Fee Structure:

    $10 per Open/Senior (20 years and over)

    $5 per Junior (14 to 19 years)

    $5 per Midget/Intermediate (13 years and under)

    Note: Uniforms, regatta fees, canoe towing and your own paddle are in addition to this fee.

    New Club Members

    Red Beach Outrigger Canoe Club (RBOCC) has a trial period for all “new paddlers” where they have 4 paddling sessions to decide whether they like the club and sport of Waka Ama (Outrigger Canoe Paddling) before they are required to join the club to continue paddling.

    Existing Club Members

    For existing club members club fees are due on the anniversary of their club membership to be paid via the clubs online portal (link).

    All membership payments are to be done through the clubs portal and any forms are to be returned to the Club Treasurer.

    ID Cards

    WANZ ID registration cards have a fee of $5.00 per paddler. (These fees are subject to change, especially at peak time, close to the Sprint Nationals where prices may increase).  These cards are a must to be able to compete in any of the Waka Ama regattas. (No ID card, no paddle).

    An electronic copy of the items below must be sent to:info@rbocc.co.nz

    1. Submit a headshot photo

    2. Provide a copy of proof of ID (copy of passport preferred, if not copy of drivers license or birth certificate)
